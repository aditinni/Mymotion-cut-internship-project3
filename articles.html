By Madhavi Venkatesan

Artificial intelligence rarely gets it wrong – it’s the culture that programmed it that’s messed up, says Madhavi Venkatesan.

In fact, our pocket artificial intelligence (AI) communicates with our other electronic devices. AI shares information across Bluetooth or Wi-Fi networks. Taking this further, given the imperfections of our society, the benefits of AI for some may continue at the persistence of marginalisation for others. AI is dependent on human programming and its learning is dependent on the quality of data. Its objectivity is fiction.
